Tag yourself!

Download the app, login, switch your gps on (for sending your coordinates), and select a tag, defining what you want to do, what you want to share, etc (e.g. #gotodinner).

Search!

Once you have entered a tag, the app will show you who is near sharing the same tag as you.

Talk!

Enter a chat room with the people with the same tag and talk to them.

FAQ

How this app works?
It's based on geolocalization, you send your position and a tag, it will show you the people with your shame tag who is near you, in a radius of 5 km.
What is the money for?
Make the app from scratch, rent a server to storage all the information, etc...
And my privacy?
You will be able to share your exact position or not (the app will say you are near, but not exactly where), and choose who do you want to talk to or who don't you want to. There will be free chat rooms, and private chat rooms whith invitation.
How I leave a chatroom/stop sending my coordinate?
Just untag you and the application will stop.
Will be more features in the future?
Maybe. I am thinking in something called "neighborhood", with permanent contacts added (independently of their position), social shares, special tags alarms, proximity offers (for retail)...
